Alana Robyn Rosen


Founder of Yarntastic Adventures

I am a life-long weirdo who navigates the world with more chronic ailments than seems appropriate. I learned to crochet from YouTube four years ago out of a strong desire to create and found something that I truly love. I’ve experimented with making hats, scarves, unicorns, seahorses, and my special Pride-O-Pusses.
In my spare time, I tend to my seven male rats whom I love a potentially unhealthy amount (I also crochet tunnels for them to play in, but they seem to be confused about the purpose and usually just shred them with adorable malice).

Navigating the world with chronic illness is a daily experiment, but between my crafts, my boys, and my disturbing sense of humor, we’re making it work several tiny rat steps at a time.
